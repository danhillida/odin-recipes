Ingredients

  • Pork Rub
  • Pork Butt
  • Barbeque Sauce

Directions

  1. Run smoker at 275 degrees
  2. Place porkbutt into smoker with water tray
  3. Spritz pork with apple cider vinegar every one to two hours
  4. When pork reaches 165 degrees, wrap
  5. When pork reaches an internal temperature of 205, remove from smoker
  6. Allow pork to rest before pulling
