Ingredients

  • 2lbs Ground Beef
  • 1 Medium Onion, Diced
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup Ketchup
  • 1 cup Panko Bread Crumbs
  • 1 tbs Soy Sauce
  • 1 tbs Worcheschur Sauce
  • 1 tbs Italian Seasoning

Steps

  1. Saute Onion until translucent.
  2. Add to large mixing bowl
  3. Add ground beef to mixing bowl
  4. Add remaining ingredients
  5. Once combined, transfer to loaf pan
  6. Bake on 350 for 50 minutes to an hour.
