Ingredients

  • 1lb Chicken Breast
  • Fajita Seasoning
  • Onion
  • Green Bell Pepper
  • Red Bell Pepper
  • Yellow Bell Pepper (Optional)
  • Lime and cilantro for garnish

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Slice chicken into thin strips, toss in bowl with fajita seasoning
  3. slice onion and peppers into strips
  4. Spread chicken and vegetables onto sheet pan, sprinkle with any reserved fajita seasoning
  5. Bake in oven for 25 minutes or until chicken reaches 160 degrees internal temperature
